Learn more about cookies

On our website, we use cookies to improve your browsing experience and offer you personalized service.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. These files allow us to collect information about your browsing habits and preferences, which helps us offer relevant content and improve our service. The cookies we use are divided into two categories: essential cookies and optional cookies. Essential cookies are necessary for the basic operation of our website and cannot be disabled. These cookies include, for example, those that allow you to log in to your account, add products to the shopping cart, or use security features.

On the other hand, optional cookies are used for analysis, personalization, and advertising purposes. These cookies allow us to collect information about how you interact with our website, such as the pages you visit most often or the products you are interested in. This information helps us improve our website and show you content and ads that are relevant to you.

Additionally, we use third-party cookies, such as Google Analytics, to collect data about traffic and user interaction on our website. These cookies are managed by third parties and are subject to their own privacy policies. It is important to note that all our cookies are completely secure and do not collect sensitive personal information, such as your name or address. However, if you prefer not to accept cookies, you can configure your browser to reject or delete them at any time. Please note that this may affect the functionality of our website and limit your browsing experience.

By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.
